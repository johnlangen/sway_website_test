# Sway Blog Content Strategy — Closing the Gap with Oakwell

Oakwell Beer Spa has 137+ blog posts creating a massive content moat. Sway currently has ~28. This document prioritizes future blog topics based on competitive analysis, search volume, and natural fit for Sway.

## Strategy

Every post should:
- List 8-14 genuine Denver recommendations (not just Sway)
- Position Sway as one of the recommendations (premium placement)
- Include 12+ internal links to booking, services, membership, gift cards
- Have FAQPage JSON-LD schema (3-5 Q&As targeting ChatGPT/AI answers)
- Be ~1,000-1,300 words with a table of contents
- Include 2-3 curated mini-itineraries that naturally end at Sway
- Cross-link to other Sway blog posts (build topical clusters)
